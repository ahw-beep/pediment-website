Our Services

Everything your property needs, managed.

We offer two core packages — tenant finding and full management — with compliance and financial reporting included as standard.

01

Tenant Finding

Finding the right tenant is the most important thing we do. A poor match costs landlords far more than an empty property — in time, stress, and money. Our process is thorough, and we don't cut corners.

We market your property across major portals, conduct accompanied viewings, carry out full reference checks, and prepare a legally compliant tenancy agreement. You'll be kept informed throughout.

  • Professional photography and listings
  • Accompanied viewings
  • Full referencing (credit, employment, previous landlord)
  • Right to Rent checks
  • Tenancy agreement preparation
  • Deposit registration
02

Full Management

Once your tenant is in, we take care of everything. Rent collection, maintenance, inspections, renewals — all handled by us, so you don't have to think about it. You remain in control of major decisions; we handle the day-to-day.

You'll have a dedicated point of contact, and your tenant will too. We keep communication professional and prompt on both sides.

  • Rent collection and arrears management
  • 24/7 maintenance coordination
  • Periodic property inspections
  • Tenancy renewals and rent reviews
  • Check-out process and deposit resolution
  • Dedicated point of contact
03

Compliance

Letting legislation changes regularly, and the consequences of non-compliance can be serious. We keep track of every legal requirement for your property so you don't have to.

From gas safety certificates to electrical condition reports, we arrange what's needed, keep records up to date, and flag upcoming renewals in advance.

  • Gas Safety Certificate (annual)
  • Electrical Installation Condition Report (EICR)
  • Energy Performance Certificate (EPC)
  • Deposit Protection Scheme registration
  • Smoke and carbon monoxide alarm compliance
  • Licensing support where applicable
04

Financial Reporting

We believe landlords should have complete clarity over the financial performance of their property. Our reporting is straightforward, consistent, and available whenever you need it.

Monthly statements are sent automatically. At year end, we provide an annual summary that makes things straightforward for your accountant.

  • Monthly income and expenditure statements
  • Annual financial summary
  • Maintenance cost records
  • Invoices and receipts for all outgoings

Not sure which service is right for you?

We're happy to talk through your situation and give you an honest recommendation.
