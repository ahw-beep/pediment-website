About Us

Built on the belief that property management should be better.

Pediment exists because landlords deserve more than the standard letting agent experience.

Our Story

Why we started Pediment

The letting industry has a reputation problem. Landlords routinely deal with poor communication, reactive maintenance, compliance oversights, and agents who treat their portfolio as an afterthought. Tenants often fare no better. It doesn't have to be this way.

Pediment was founded on a straightforward belief: that property management done well — with clear standards, consistent communication, and genuine care — produces better outcomes for everyone involved. Better relationships between landlords and tenants. Fewer voids, fewer disputes, and properties that hold their value.

We work with a deliberately manageable number of properties so that every landlord receives proper attention. We'd rather do fewer things well than spread ourselves too thin.

The name comes from architecture — the pediment is the triangular structure above a row of columns, the defining mark of a building built to last. That's the standard we hold ourselves to.

What We Stand For

Our values

These aren't aspirations — they're the way we work, every day.

Honesty

We tell landlords what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. If there's a problem, you'll know about it promptly and with a clear plan.

Reliability

We do what we say we'll do, when we say we'll do it. Simple as that. In an industry where follow-through is rare, we make it a baseline.

Care

We manage properties as though they were our own. That means attention to detail, swift responses, and taking pride in the condition and performance of every home.

Clarity

No jargon, no hidden fees, no surprises. Everything we do is explained clearly — from our pricing to our processes.
